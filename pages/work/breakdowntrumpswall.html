Break Down Trump's Wall

Break Down Trump's Wall is an Android mobile game developed using MIT App Inventor, a block-based visual programming enviorment. The game was created just after the 2016 election of Donald Trump as a comedic commentary on the proposed border wall between the United States and Mexico that had become a hugely controversial theme in the President's campaign and presidency. The game is a "Brick Breaker" style game where the player moves a paddle along the bottom edge of the screen in order to bounce a ball and break each piece of brick. The player wins when they have broken down enough pieces to hit the image of Donald Trump and is rewarded with having "saved America".


PROCESS

The application utilizes a simple design including a red, white, and blue color scheme to represent the colors of the American flag. The game includes elements such as sprites, a countdown clock so the game ends in 60 seconds, and intuitive gameplay. It can be downloaded to any andorid device using the QR code below.
