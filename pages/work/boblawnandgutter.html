Bob's Lawn and Gutter

click to play Bob's Lawn and Gutter

Bob's Lawn and Gutter is a series of three minigames inspired by my dad, Bob. When I was growing up, my dad owned a lawn care buisness called Bob Lawn and Gutter where he would take care of people's lawns around our town. Bob is also the one who introduced me to video games, particular the 8-bit style games he used to play as a kid like Galaga and Dig Dug. This series of video games, created using p5.js, is a tribute to him and his love for video games (and lawncare).
