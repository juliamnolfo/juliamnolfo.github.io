Line Portraits

In this series of portraits, I experimented with using differnt line widths, styles, and pens to draw portraits. The different techiniques give each drawing a unique qualitiy and mood, managing to exaggerate different features. Though this medium is atypical for me, I enjoyed the greatly enjoyed the process of creating these portraits and stepping outside of my comfort zone. I am quite proud of the end result.
