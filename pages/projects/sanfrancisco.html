Scenes of San Francisco

Since moving to San Francisco in 2016, I have fallen in love with the city and find beauty everywhere I go. Scenes of San Francisco a series of photographs taken over several years that depict my favorite places around the city and are laden with memories from my time living here, from the first time I saw the ocean, college nights spent exploring downtown, and the most jawdropping views I've ever seen. I've even been blessed to have friends use some of these images as desktop backgrounds!
