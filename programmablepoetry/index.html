programmable_poetry

programmable_poetry is a collaborative poetry writing experience between human and computer, emphasizing the strengths of each party. computers are efficient at performing complex computations quickly while humans have a unique knowledge of creativity and emotion. choose a poet from the buttons below to get started writing with programmable_poetry.

select from the randomly generated words on the left in order to create a poem on the right. click "generate" to generate more words, "undo" to undo the last action, and "line break" to insert a line break, click "finish" when you are finished.
