Step 4

Next, we make the gooey part. In a bowl, add cream cheese in chunks, vanilla extract, the remainder of the butter, and the last two eggs. Beat this mixture with an electric mixer on medium speed until the cream cheese is softened and it’s fully mixed. (If you’re a broke college student like I am, you can get your daily exercise in by beating the mixture by hand. It gives you more time to think about the insane amounts of butter you just put into this cake)
