Step 8

When you first take it out of the oven, it's going to seem like it isn't done because it'll be jiggly when you move the pan. But, as long as it's golden brown on top, you've done it correctly. Allow it to cool completely. If you don’t do this, the top layer will not settle completely and it’ll be impossible to cut. (Seriously. It'll be like Jello Gooey Butter Cake and I think we can all agree that sounds gross)
