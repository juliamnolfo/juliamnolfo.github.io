If you feel like you’ve conquered the Gateway to the West and you’re ready for the next great challenge or you’ve been eating gooey butter cake for every meal for the last week and you’re ready for a change, try these variations.

Pumpkin Gooey Butter Cake

Add 1 can of pumpkin, 1 teaspoon of nutmeg, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon into the top layer of the cake before mixing in the powdered sugar. Serve with whipped cream on top.

Banana Gooey Butter Cake

Add two rip and sliced bananas into the filling before beating in the powdered sugar.

Peanut Butter Gooey Butter Cake

Replace the yellow cake mix for chocolate cake mix. Add I cup of creamy peanut butter before mixing in the powdered sugar.

Choclate Chip Gooey Butter Cake

Add 1 cup of chocolate chips on top of
the cake before placing it in the oven.

Red Velvet Gooey Butter Cake

Use red velevet cake mix instead of yellow. Serve with white frosting on top.
