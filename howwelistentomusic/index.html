How We Listen to Music

This series explores the different ways and mediums through which we as humans have consumed music over time.

Record Player

Click in the record player's needle to let it down onto the record and play the song. Click it again to lift the needle.

Live Music

First, choose a major chord by pressing and holding either "a", "b", "c", "d", "e", "f", or "g" on your keyboard. Then, click and drag your mouse over the strings of the guitar to hear the chord played.
