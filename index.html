ABOUT JULIA

Julia Nolfo is a graphic designer based in San Francisco, California. She grew up in St. Louis, Missouri but moved to SF in 2016 to attend the University of San Francisco and will recieve her BA majoring in Design and Minoring in Computer Science in May 2020. Julia has a particular interest in UI/UX and Human-Computer Interaction, the perfect blend between Design and Computer Science. She is also an experienced Theatre Technician, Lighting Designer, and has passions in Computer Science Education and Photography. Julia has a twin sister, Cameron, and was born on Christmas Eve.
