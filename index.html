A Bit About Me...

Hi! I’m Julia Nolfo (she/her), a designer based in San Francisco, California, though I'm a Midwesterner at heart—originally from St. Louis, Missouri. I made the move to San Francisco to earn my Bachelor of Arts in Design with a minor in Computer Science from the University of San Francisco in 2020. I've developed a strong interest for areas like Typography, Publication Design, and Human-Computer Interaction.

In addition to my design work, I’m lucky enough to share my love for education as an Adjunct Professor of Computer Science at USF. I also have a background in Theatre, where I’ve worked as a Lighting Designer and Technician. On a more personal note, I’m a twin—my sister, Cameron, and I were born on Christmas Eve and our first tattoos were each other's names! ♡

I’m always looking to connect with other creatives and educators, so feel free to reach out!.
